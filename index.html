Jo Hotel

Jo hotel is a small boutique hotel located in Langkawi, Kedah, Malaysia, surrounded by charming tropical rainforest, beautiful and vivid coral reefs and mangroves

Book Online

In Jo hotel, wake up in the morning, and you can swim in the hotel's small swimming pool and enjoy the hotel's special local-style breakfast (please tick the breakfast required when ordering the room).
After recuperating, explore the mysterious mangroves and coast, visit and observe a landscape different from the city built with steel and concrete.
Our wish is to bring unforgettable and relaxing memories to all our guests.

About Hotel

Jo Hotel takes the green jungle as the design concept, hoping to create a safe and reliable accommodation process that brings green atmosphere and close contact with nature.
The swimming pool and gym are open all day, except for the cleaning time from 12 to 3 in the morning, which is inconvenient to use.
Please shower in the adjacent shower room before entering the swimming pool. We hope to bring a comfortable environment to all customers.

The area of Economy Rooms are 37 m².
The area of Medium Rooms are 45 m².
The area of Queen Rooms are 70 m².

All rooms are open all year round. Check-in time is from 4:00 to 6:00, if you will check in later, please don't forget to choose your check-in time on the reservation page, or you can contact us for help.

There are car parks on both sides of the hotel, free of charge for all customers.

You can rebook any room you want until 12 o'clock the day before your arrival. But we apologize, once you book a room, the system cannot cancel it for free, but if it is a special event, please contact us and let us refund you manually.

The swimming pool is open all day, wishing to heal the fatigue of the journey.

Occasional exercise can also be part of reducing stress while traveling.
